Avocado Toast

Cooking time: 5-15 minutes
Servings: 2-4

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado, mashed
  • 2 poached eggs
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Feta cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread until lightly browned
  2. Spread the mashed avocado on top
  3. Place the poached eggs on top of the avocado.
  4. add the cherry tomatoes and sprinkle with salt, pepper, and feta
    cheese (if using).
  5. Serve and enjoy!
