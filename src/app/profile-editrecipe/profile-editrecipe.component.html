Afang Soup


Porridge Yam


Porridge Yam

Preparation time: 20 minutes

- Cooking time: 25-30 minutes

- Total time: 45-50 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 medium yam tuber
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 large tomato
  • 1 habanero pepper
  • 1 large onion
  • 3 cups water
  • 1/2 cup fresh palm oil
  • 2 tablespoons crayfish
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons chicken bouillon powder
  • 15 ounces fish (optional)
  • Leafy vegetables (optional)

Instruction:

  1. Peel and cut the yam tuber into large chunks and rinse them until the water runs clear.
  2. In a blender, combine the bell pepper, habanero pepper, tomato and half of the onion.
  3. In a large pot, combine the yams, the blended pepper mix, salt, bouillon powder and water.
  4. Cover and cook the mixture on medium to high heat for 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in the palm oil, crayfish and the rest of the onions.
  6. Cover the pot again and cook the mixture for another 10 minutes.
  7. Mash the yams with a potato masher or a large serving spoon. Leave some chunks for texture.
  8. Add fish and simmer the mixture for 3 to 5 minutes.
  9. Stir in the herbs and leafy greens.
  10. Serve the dish while it's still hot.
